corgi.js

A corgi that follows your mouse cursor around the page. Based on oneko.js.

Move your mouse around — the corgi will chase it! Leave the cursor still and watch the corgi perform idle animations (tail wag, bark, beg, sleep...).

Usage

<script src="corgi.js"></script>

Custom sprite:

<script src="corgi.js" data-corgi="./my-corgi.png"></script>
